My Story

My story starts when I was a teenager.
Not understanding nutrition I made bad eating choices, and rapidly started putting on weight. By the time I was 14 years old, I weighed a massive 260 pounds (that is 130kg). I was contantly tormented by the other kids. And then to top it off I got diagnosed with Advanced Type 2 Diabetes. I was also told that my foot would have to be amputated due to the Diabetes.


This was devastating to me and I decided enough was enough. It was a long road to recovery but now I am of normal weight, am off all Diabetes medication and living a healthy life. Low carb eating gave me my life back.

Enjoy these recipes and resources and enjoy a healthy life!
