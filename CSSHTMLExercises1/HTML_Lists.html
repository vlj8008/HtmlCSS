The basic procedure for making kombucha is:

  1. Brew tea on the stove at a boil for minimum of one hour.
  2. Place in fermenting container with a healthy SCOBY, cover and leave in the dark for at least one week.
  3. Remove SCOBY and place tea in bottles. Place in a dark area.
  4. After one week, kombucha is ready for drinking.

    5. The alcohol content of kombucha depends on a number of factors such as:

    • The tea used to make the kombucha
    • The health of the SCOBY
    • The brewing time.
    • The time between brewing and drinking.
